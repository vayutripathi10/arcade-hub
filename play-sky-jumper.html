🚀 Play Sky Jumper Online

Sky Jumper is a high-velocity vertical platform jumper set in a glowing cyber-sky. Double-jump off moving platforms, dodge incoming tracker rockets, bounce on high-power spring coils, and grab shields to climb to the stars. Featuring deep vector graphics, interactive combos, and a custom synthesizer audio track, it's an addictive test of reactions.

🕹️ Visible Gameplay & Controls Guide

Move left and right using the A/D or Arrow keys on desktop, or tilt your phone (gyroscope) / drag your finger on mobile touchscreens to steer. Jumps are handled automatically on platform contacts.

💡 Expert Tips & Pro Strategies

Dodge rockets at the last second to trigger a 'Near Miss' speed burst. Prioritize collecting glowing forcefields; they shield you from one rocket strike and magnetize nearby score tokens.

⚡ Cybernetic Specifications

  • Rendering Core: HTML5 Canvas / 2D WebGL execution vectors.
  • Framerate Limits: Locked 60 FPS up to fluid 120 FPS high-refresh capabilities.
  • Audio Synthesizer: Browser Web Audio API oscillator nodes for live programmatic synthesizer soundtrack rendering.
  • Offline Capabilities: Progressive Web App caching via local Service Workers — 100% playable offline!

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does it support tilt controls on mobile?

A: Yes! Sky Jumper supports both touch-dragging and device gyroscope tilting. You can toggle your preferred controls in the game's start menu.

Q: Are my scores saved globally?

A: High scores are saved locally inside your browser's secure data files and displayed on your local leaderboard in the Trophy Room.

Q: Is the game sound synthesized dynamically?

A: Yes, using the Web Audio API, the game generates all bounces, rocket alerts, and forcefield hums programmatically in real-time.
