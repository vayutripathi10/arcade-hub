Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 20, 2026

Welcome to Arcade Hub. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require users to create accounts or provide personal information such as names or email addresses to play our games. However, we may collect certain non-identifiable information automatically:

  • Usage Data: We use Google Analytics to collect data on how users interact with the site (e.g., pages visited, time spent on games).
  • Cookies: Small files stored on your device to help analyze web traffic and improve your experience.

2. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Analytics: To understand site traffic and user behavior.
  • Google AdSense: We use Google AdSense to serve ads. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

3. Children's Privacy

Arcade Hub is built for everyone, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

4. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach out via our GitHub project page.
