⚔️ Play Stick Duel Towers Online

Stick Duel Towers is a strategic, physics-based vertical shooter. Climb glowing cybersecurity tower rooms, battle enemy stick figures, collect weapon packages, and increase your combat stats. Master momentum, recoil physics, and bullet trajectories to clear every floor and claim the server core.

🕹️ Visible Gameplay & Controls Guide

Use WASD to move and jump. Aim and fire using your mouse pointer on desktop. On mobile, use dual virtual joysticks to move and shoot dynamically.

💡 Expert Tips & Pro Strategies

Use the recoil of heavy weapons (like shotguns) to propel yourself across wide gaps. Aim for headshots to instantly disable high-health tower guards.

⚡ Cybernetic Specifications

  • Rendering Core: HTML5 Canvas / 2D WebGL execution vectors.
  • Framerate Limits: Locked 60 FPS up to fluid 120 FPS high-refresh capabilities.
  • Audio Synthesizer: Browser Web Audio API oscillator nodes for live programmatic synthesizer soundtrack rendering.
  • Offline Capabilities: Progressive Web App caching via local Service Workers — 100% playable offline!

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are there multiple weapon types?

A: Yes! Collect pistols, automatic rifles, shotguns, rocket launchers, and glowing energy sabers inside the tower crates.

Q: Can I play in 2-player mode?

A: Stick Duel Towers is optimized for solo campaign runs and local hotseat multiplayer. Connect two mechs on one keyboard for epic battles!

Q: Is the physics engine responsive?

A: Yes. The custom vector physics engine recalculates gravity and recoil vectors instantly, ensuring realistic, high-speed movement.
