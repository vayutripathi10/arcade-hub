🚀 Play Neon Space Shooter Online

Neon Space Shooter is an arcade vertical space shooter. Fly your glowing starship through deep space grids, dodge meteors, and blast alien interceptors. Gather power-up crates to upgrade your weapon arrays from single blasters to triple laser cannons and wide-angle energy beams.

🕹️ Visible Gameplay & Controls Guide

Drag your mouse or finger on mobile screens to fly your ship. Firing is fully automatic, letting you focus on navigation and dodging.

💡 Expert Tips & Pro Strategies

Position your ship near the bottom-center of the screen. This gives you the maximum reaction time to dodge high-speed meteors and alien lasers.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do weapons downgrade if I get hit?

A: No, your weapon upgrades last throughout your run. Getting hit decreases your shield, which can be restored by picking up blue shield icons.

Q: How do I activate the super-laser?

A: Gathering three consecutive green power-up gems triggers a temporary 'Hyper Beam' that instantly vaporizes all enemies in your path.

Q: Is there high-score saving?

A: Yes, your high scores and total flight time are saved locally inside your browser's data profile.
