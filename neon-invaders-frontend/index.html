About Neon Invaders

Defend the cyber-grid from descending waves of retro geometric alien invaders in Neon Invaders, a premium homage to the classic Space Invaders. Pilot your glowing defense cannon, fire plasma bolts, and utilize destructible neon barriers to shield yourself from incoming heavy energy blasts. Complete with particle explosions and a pounding synth track, this is classic space combat at its finest.

🕹️ Complete Game Controls Guide

On Desktop: Use the Left and Right Arrow Keys to slide your cannon. Press Spacebar to shoot at the descending invaders.

On Mobile: Tap the left and right directional arrows at the bottom of the screen to move, and tap the Blast button on the right to shoot.

💡 Pro Tips & Strategies for High Scores

  • Use the shields: Destructible neon walls block enemy fire. Duck behind them to reload or dodge heavy incoming alien barrages.
  • Pick off the edges: Focus on the leftmost and rightmost alien columns first. This slows down their descent speed across the screen.
  • Target the mother ship: Occasionally, a high-value red alien ship will fly across the very top. Hit it for massive bonus points.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Do the invaders get faster?
A: Yes! As the number of invaders decreases, the remaining space aliens move and descend at significantly faster speeds.
Q: Is the game endless?
A: The game features multiple waves. Cleared waves lead to faster, more aggressive alien fleets with enhanced firing rates.
