Neon Invaders

Neon Invaders is a premium cyberpunk reimagining of the classic Space Invaders arcade game. Command a glowing laser cannon at the bottom of the screen and blast waves of descending neon alien invaders before they reach your base. Dodge enemy fire, destroy alien formations row by row, and face increasingly aggressive waves with boss enemies that require multiple hits to eliminate. Pure retro shooting action with modern neon visuals.

🎮 How to Play

Move your cannon left and right and shoot upward to destroy all invaders in each wave. Invaders move faster as their numbers decrease. Reach wave 5 to face the first boss encounter.

  • Desktop — Move: Left / Right Arrow Keys or A / D keys
  • Desktop — Shoot: Spacebar
  • Mobile: Drag to move (ship fires automatically)
  • Goal: Clear every wave without letting invaders reach the bottom

⚡ Game Features

  • Classic formation-based alien waves
  • Shield barriers you can hide behind (and aliens destroy)
  • UFO bonus ships flying across the top for huge point bonuses
  • Boss alien waves every 5 levels with unique attack patterns

🏆 Pro Tips

  • Always shoot the bottom rows of invaders first — they are closest to your base
  • Hide behind shields to bait enemy fire, then emerge to return shots
  • Shoot UFO ships when they appear — they give massive score multipliers
  • As invaders thin out, they speed up dramatically — stay mobile
