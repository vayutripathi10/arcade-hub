📖 How to Play

🎯 Goal: Hit the opponent's head with your stick to deal damage.

🕹️ Controls: Hold SPACE or Touch & Hold the screen to grow your stick upward.

📏 Aiming: Release when the stick is the right length to reach the opponent.

🎯 Headshot: Direct head hit = 30 damage + slow-mo effect!

💪 Body Hit: Stick lands on the body = 10 damage.

❌ Miss: Too short or too long = 0 damage!

🌬️ Wind: Watch the wind indicator in later levels — it shifts your stick!

🏆 Win: Reduce your opponent's health to 0 first.
