Neon Helix Drop

Neon Helix Drop is a hypnotic, reflex-driven arcade game where you rotate a glowing neon helix tower and guide a falling ball safely through the gaps in each rotating ring. The ball drops continuously — you must spin the tower left or right to align the gaps before it hits a solid platform. Miss a gap and you lose. Survive long enough and the tower spins faster, demanding lightning-fast reactions.

🎮 How to Play

The ball falls from the top. Rotate the helix tower to line up the gaps in each ring with the ball's falling path. The ball passes safely through gaps but shatters on solid ring segments.

  • Desktop — Rotate Left: Left Arrow key or A key
  • Desktop — Rotate Right: Right Arrow key or D key
  • Mobile: Swipe left or right on the screen to rotate the tower
  • Goal: Survive as many rings as possible without hitting solid segments

⚡ Game Features

  • Endless helix tower with randomized gap patterns
  • Progressive speed increase as you descend deeper
  • Satisfying neon particle burst on successful passes
  • Global high-score tracking via local storage

🏆 Pro Tips

  • Watch 2–3 rings ahead, not just the current one
  • Small, precise taps beat wild spinning — overrotating loses time
  • At high speeds, pre-rotate to the anticipated gap position early
  • Stay calm: panic spins cause more misses than the speed itself
