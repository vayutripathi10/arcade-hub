Neon Zombie Shooter

Welcome to Neon Zombie Shooter, a fast-paced top-down 3D survival game. Locked inside a high-tech neon security grid, you must hold off hordes of viral cybernetic zombies, manage your ammunition caches, and navigate through obstacles to reach the glowing destination extraction portal.

🎮 How to Play

Move around the grid and aim at incoming zombies. When you clear the path, locate the glowing exit portal and step on it to advance levels.

  • WASD / Arrow Keys: Move character
  • Mouse Move: Aim weapon
  • Left-Click / Space: Shoot weapon
  • Shift / Right-Click: Cyber Dash (fast dodge with cooldown)
  • Mobile Controls: Use the left virtual joystick to move, and touch/drag the right joystick to aim and shoot automatically!

⚡ Survival Upgrades

  • Collect glowing Blue Ammo boxes to reload.
  • Pick up Green Nano-Medkits to restore your health.
  • Build high scores and survival streaks across levels.
  • Use your dash strategically to escape corner traps.

🏆 Pro Tips

  • Don't stand still! Keep moving to kite the zombie horde.
  • Zombies spawn from vents. Clear the area around spawners first.
  • Dashing makes you invulnerable for a split second. Use it to phase through enemy bodies.
  • Watch the minimap arrow in the top right to locate the exit.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Does this game require high-end graphics cards?

No. It uses low-poly optimized geometries and custom shaders running on Three.js, ensuring native 60 FPS performance on even mid-range mobile browsers.

Is Neon Zombie Shooter fully free?

Yes, 100% free with no registration or installations. It works fully offline once cached.
