Space Shooter

Space Shooter is a classic vertical scrolling shoot-em-up with a neon cyberpunk visual skin. Pilot your starfighter through the cosmos, blasting waves of alien spacecraft, dodging enemy fire, and collecting power-ups to upgrade your weapons. Every few waves introduces a massive boss ship with unique attack phases. A love letter to the golden era of arcade space shooters, rebuilt for modern browsers.

🎮 How to Play

Fly your ship upward through scrolling space and shoot all enemy ships. Dodge enemy bullets and collect dropped power-ups. Reach the end of each wave to trigger a boss fight.

  • Desktop — Move: Arrow Keys or WASD
  • Desktop — Shoot: Spacebar (auto-fire available in settings)
  • Mobile: Touch and drag your ship to move; auto-fire is enabled
  • Goal: Survive all waves and defeat every boss

⚡ Game Features

  • Multiple weapon upgrades: spread shot, laser, missiles
  • Formation-based enemy waves with coordinated attack patterns
  • Boss ships with multiple attack phases
  • Shield pick-ups and health restore drops

🏆 Pro Tips

  • Collect weapon power-ups immediately — upgraded firepower is decisive
  • Stay near the bottom of the screen to maximise reaction time to enemy bullets
  • Target the edges of formations first to reduce incoming fire volume
  • During boss fights, focus on attack pattern recognition over aggressive shooting
