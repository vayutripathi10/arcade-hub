📖 How to Play
🕹️Controls: Use Arrow Keys / WASD to move. On mobile, drag to steer.
🔫Auto-Fire: Your ship shoots automatically — focus on dodging!
👽Enemies: Destroy Scout, Battle, and heavy Tank alien ships.
🎁Power-ups: Collect drops from Tank aliens for shields and firepower.
🏆Goal: Survive the alien waves and score as high as possible!

About Neon Space Shooter

Take command of a futuristic space fighter in Neon Space Shooter, a fast-paced shoot-'em-up. Blast your way through asteroid fields and fleets of alien ships. Collect powerful weapon upgrades, shields, and laser arrays to maximize your firepower. With smooth dragging controls and bright particle effects, it's a stellar arcade experience.

🕹️ Complete Game Controls Guide

On Desktop: Move your mouse cursor to smoothly guide your spaceship across the screen. Firing is automatic and rapid.

On Mobile: Touch and drag anywhere on the screen to guide your ship. The spaceship will follow your finger and fire automatically.

💡 Pro Tips & Strategies for High Scores

  • Focus on dodging: Your ship fires automatically. Focus your eyes on incoming hostile lasers and steer your ship safely around them.
  • Collect laser upgrades: Blue and red power-ups upgrade your standard blaster into wide triple-lasers and heavy plasma beams.
  • Utilize the shield: The glowing shield power-up absorbs one hit from any asteroid or laser, saving your life in crowded screens.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I upgrade weapons in Neon Space Shooter?
A: Upgrade icons float across the screen during combat. Fly your ship over these icons to upgrade your blaster and activate secondary weapons.
Q: Is there a final boss?
A: Yes! Face off against massive alien mother ships at the end of each sector, featuring bullet-hell patterns and heavy shields.
