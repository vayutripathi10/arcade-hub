About Neon Stick Fighter

Master retro-cyber martial arts in Neon Stick Fighter. Battle endless hordes of incoming stickman fighters in a side-scrolling neon city. Unleash punches, kicks, and devastating combos to clear the grid. With highly fluid action frames, glowing particle impacts, and challenging boss fights, Neon Stick Fighter is the ultimate arcade brawler.

🕹️ Complete Game Controls Guide

On Desktop: Press the Left and Right Arrow Keys to move. Press Down Arrow to kick and punch. Combos execute automatically with rapid presses.

On Mobile: Use the left virtual d-pad to move left or right. Tap the large Strike button on the right to attack.

💡 Pro Tips & Strategies for High Scores

  • Execute combos: Rapidly press the attack button to chain simple moves into heavy roundhouse kicks and high-damage punches.
  • Keep enemies in front of you: Getting surrounded is deadly. Push enemies away using your basic kick before turning to fight others.
  • Prepare for Bosses: Every 10 standard foes defeated will spawn a massive boss. Conserve your health and time your attacks to beat them.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I trigger special attacks in Neon Stick Fighter?
A: Chain 3 or more strikes in rapid succession to trigger the glowing roundhouse kick combo, which knocks down multiple enemies at once.
