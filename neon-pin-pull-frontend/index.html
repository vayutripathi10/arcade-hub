Neon Pin Pull

Neon Pin Pull is a glowing physics-based puzzle arcade game where you steer colored neon balls through gravity-defying chambers to match their corresponding bottom buckets. Unlatch walls, slide barriers, and coordinate pin-pulling paths in the correct sequence to guide balls safely. One wrong sequence and colors mix, causing system overrides and level failure. Exercise spatial timing and strategic planning to solve all cybersecurity cores!

🎮 How to Play

Pull barriers strategically. Balls behave as realistic gravity entities. Reach matching color buckets to decrypt nodes.

  • Pull Pin: Tap/Click the pin's arrow indicator.
  • Directional Motion: Pins only pull in the direction of their arrows.
  • Chain Locks: Blocked pins shake red and cannot be pulled until overlapping pins are removed.
  • Avoid Mixing: Guiding balls to incorrect buckets or mixing colors causes instant failure.

⚡ Game Features

  • Smooth, real-time vector gravity ball kinematics
  • Shaking indicators and lock markers for blocked barriers
  • Real-time Web Audio synthesized whooshes and chimes
  • 20 progressive logic mainframe encryption levels

🏆 Strategy Tips

  • Trace the bottom goal buckets upwards to find the required sequence.
  • Pins blocked by others glow red—pull the blocking pin first!
  • Time your pulls to group same-colored balls together inside intermediate chambers.
  • In Hard/Expert levels, look for chain dependencies where pulling one pin unlocks another.

Level fail triggers if any ball lands in a wrong-colored bucket or if colors mix together inside any goal bucket.
