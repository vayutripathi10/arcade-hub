🏹 Play Neon Arrows Online

Neon Arrows is a premium, spatial reasoning logic puzzle game where you are tasked with clearing a security sector grid. Decrypt the glowing cybernetic locks by peeling away paths of color-coded vectors. Each path is a line winding through the grid dots, ending in a directional arrowhead. Select and slide paths off the board in the correct sequence to prevent lockups and decrypt the core database!

🕹️ Gameplay & Controls Guide

Click or tap on any glowing path to slide it in the direction of its arrowhead. The path will trace its bends and exit straight off the screen. If another line occupies any cell of its exit trajectory, it will collide and wiggle, meaning you must clear the obstacle first.

💡 Expert Tips & Pro Strategies

Begin by scanning the outer rows and columns. Lines that exit directly without crossing other paths are your key starting points. Work backwards from blocked heads to identify which dependency lines must be cleared first. Use Undo if you make an incorrect slide!

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are the level solutions unique?

A: Each hand-crafted puzzle has a specific logical order of clearance. However, some minor non-blocking lines can be removed at multiple points in the sequence.

Q: What is a "Perfect Run"?

A: Every level has a "Perfect" target indicator showing the minimum number of moves required to solve the puzzle. Completing a level within this move limit unlocks the "Perfect" trophy badge.

Q: Is there any time limit?

A: No! Neon Arrows is designed as a relaxing, cozy puzzle game. You have all the time you need to think through and plan your moves without pressure.
