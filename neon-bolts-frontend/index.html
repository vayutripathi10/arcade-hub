Neon Screws & Bolts

Neon Screws & Bolts is an immersive, physics-based spatial puzzle game. Tap on glowing cybernetic bolts to unscrew them and move them into empty holes to release translucent hanging vector plates. As plates drop down from the gravity grid, you clear layers and reveal new secure terminal nodes. Plan your steps carefully—with limited free holes, every single move shifts the center of mass and changes the pendulum swings of hanging plates!

🎮 How to Play

Unscrew and shift bolts to let all vector plates drop completely off the screen.

  • Select Bolt: Tap/Click any occupied bolt to select it. Selected bolts glow and float slightly.
  • Place Bolt: Tap/Click any unoccupied hole to place the selected bolt.
  • Drop Plates: A plate is completely locked if held by 2 or more bolts. If held by only 1 bolt, it rotates freely around it under gravity. If it has 0 bolts, it falls off the screen!
  • Goal: Make all plates fall off the board to unlock the secure CPU core and clear the level.

⚡ Cybernetic Features

  • Realistic 2D pendulum rotation & gravity physics.
  • Translucent glassmorphism plates showing overlapping depths.
  • Satisfying dynamic real-time synthesized sound effects.
  • Sleek, fluid neon particle explosion feedback.

🏆 Hacker Pro Strategies

  • Use pivots to your advantage! Letting a plate rotate down can uncover occupied bolts hiding underneath.
  • Avoid trapping your only empty holes under falling plates.
  • Clear the outermost overlapping layers first.
  • Study the pivot centers to predict which way a plate will swing.
