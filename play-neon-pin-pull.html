📌 Play Neon Pin Pull Online

Neon Pin Pull is an addictive, physics-based neon puzzle game. Solve challenging brain teasers by pulling pins in the correct order to guide glowing colored balls into matching goal buckets. Features 20 progressive difficulty levels, intricate interlocks, moving funnels, time-pressure chambers, and dynamic vector gravity stacking. Play online in your browser for free, completely offline-compatible with mobile touch responsive layouts.

🕹️ Visible Gameplay & Controls Guide

To interact with the mainframe, hover and click (or tap on mobile) any pin's directional arrow cap. Pins can only slide in the direction indicated by their arrow. If a pin is blocked by another, it will glow bright red and play a thud sound with a visual shake indicator.

💡 Expert Tips & Pro Strategies

Approach every stage by working backwards from the bottom goals. Look at the colors of the bottom buckets and trace which chambers contain the corresponding spheres. Identify chain dependencies—some pins must be held in place to block certain colors from mixing.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I unlock new levels?

A: You unlock levels progressively as you successfully decrypt the previous core. You can also view the Level Grid in the start menu to replay any previously completed level to optimize your star ratings.

Q: What causes a level to fail instantly?

A: A stage fails if any ball lands in a goal bucket of a different color, or if balls of different colors mix together inside a bucket. In time-pressure stages, running out of time also triggers a failure.

Q: Can I share my high scores on social media?

A: Yes! When you complete a level, you can click 'Share on X' or 'WhatsApp' to share your level completion status and star ratings with friends.
