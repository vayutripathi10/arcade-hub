About Sky Jumper

Sky Jumper is a high-flying, neon-infused vertical bouncing game. Bounce from platform to platform to climb into the stratosphere. Target bouncy springs, avoid crumbling steps, and dodge incoming flying hazards in this highly addictive vertical adventure featuring smooth physics and beautiful particle trails.

🕹️ Complete Game Controls Guide

On Desktop: Use the Left and Right Arrow Keys (or A/D) to tilt and steer the jumper. Bouncing is automatic.

On Mobile: Tilt your smartphone left and right to guide the jumper (uses device accelerometer) or tap the left and right screen borders.

💡 Pro Tips & Strategies for High Scores

  • Aim for springs: Neon springs provide massive vertical boosts, bypassing several screen heights of tricky platforms instantly.
  • Avoid crumbling clouds: Some white platforms crumble immediately after you land on them. Make sure you have a backup platform in mind.
  • Dodge retro spikes: Spikes and flying rockets spawn on later stages. Tilt quickly to steer clear of these deadly hazards.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How high can I climb in Sky Jumper?
A: The sky is the limit! The game is endless, featuring unique procedural layouts that grow more challenging as you climb higher.
Q: What happens if I fall?
A: Falling below the bottom of the screen ends the game. Aim for safe platforms or springs to keep your climb active.
