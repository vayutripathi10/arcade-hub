Sky Jumper

Sky Jumper is a high-flying, neon-infused vertical platformer where your goal is to leap upward through an endless, procedurally-generated world of floating platforms, glowing springs, and deadly hazards. You control a nimble neon character who must jump, double-jump, and dodge missiles and spike traps as the world scrolls ever faster beneath your feet. With buttery-smooth physics, vibrant particle effects, and increasingly wild platform patterns, Sky Jumper is an addictive test of reflexes and spatial awareness that keeps you coming back to beat your personal best.

🎮 How to Play

Your character automatically bounces upward. Use movement controls to steer left and right, and jump to boost higher off platforms. The double-jump is your best friend — use it to recover from near-misses and reach distant platforms.

  • Desktop — Move: Left / Right Arrow Keys or A / D keys
  • Desktop — Jump: Spacebar, Up Arrow, or W key
  • Mobile — Move: Tap the left or right side of the screen
  • Mobile — Jump: Tap the jump button on the virtual D-pad

⚡ Game Features

  • Endless procedurally-generated vertical worlds
  • Double-jump mechanic for advanced platform recovery
  • Neon spring platforms that launch you skyward
  • Rockets and spike traps that increase in difficulty

🏆 Pro Tips

  • Always aim for neon spring platforms — they give massive height boosts
  • Watch for crumbling white platforms and have a backup landing ready
  • Save your double-jump for when you overshoot a platform
  • Center your position on the screen to react to both sides quickly
