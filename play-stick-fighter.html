🥷 Play Neon Stick Fighter Online

Neon Stick Fighter is a fluid side-scrolling martial arts brawler set in a neon-lit cyberpunk city. Battle waves of incoming stickman fighters using punches, kicks, and powerful combo chains. Each enemy staggers, stumbles, and reacts with ragdoll physics to your attacks. Progress through increasingly dangerous fighter types including armored elites and rapid dual-weapon attackers, culminating in giant stage bosses that test your combat timing.

🕹️ Visible Gameplay & Controls Guide

Use Left/Right Arrow Keys or A/D on desktop to move left and right. Jump using the Up Arrow, W, or Spacebar. Trigger attacks using the Down Arrow Key, S key, or by tapping the on-screen virtual controls on mobile devices.

Combat relies on a progressive combo register. Tapping the attack input consecutively chains strikes together: Jab → Kick → Uppercut → slam finisher. Uppercut attacks launch enemies into the air, allowing you to juggle them or execute jumping jump slams for high impact area damage.

💡 Expert Tips & Pro Strategies

Avoid getting surrounded by rolling or jumping over enemies to keep them all on one side. Keep an eye out for boss milestones—defeated bosses drop a physical Neon Sword that deals high damage for 15 hits.

In later stages, armored guards will block standard Jabs. Use the kick finisher (hit 2 and 3 of the combo chain) to break their guard and knock them back, creating critical space. Watch the screen edges for weapon spawns, as picking up the glowing orange sword increases your strike range and damage significantly.

🥋 Combat Combo System & Finishers

Mastering the four-stage combat combo chain is essential to survive past Stage 5:

  • Combo Hit 1 (Jab): A fast close-range punch that staggers the target, dealing 5 damage.
  • Combo Hit 2 (Roundhouse): A wider-range kick that pushes enemies back, dealing 10 damage and creating distance.
  • Combo Hit 3 (Uppercut): An upward strike that launches the enemy into the air, dealing 15 damage. Highly effective for locking down single elites.
  • Combo Hit 4 (Slam Finisher): A heavy jumping overhead strike that slams the target to the ground, dealing 25 damage and creating a shockwave that knocks back surrounding guards.

A: Yes! Unique boss guards spawn at the end of each stage. They feature custom neon colors, visual accessories (hats, horns, mohawks), and massive health pools.

Q: Does it support mobile controllers?

A: Yes, virtual touchscreen move buttons, jump, and strike buttons are fully supported and display automatically on mobile devices.
