Neon Retro Racer

Neon Retro Racer is a high-speed top-down racing game inspired by classic 80s arcade racers. Weave your glowing neon vehicle through dense traffic on an endlessly scrolling neon highway. Avoid crashes, draft behind rivals to build boost, and collect fuel pickups to extend your run. The road gets faster and traffic denser the further you drive — how long can you stay on the road?

🎮 How to Play

Steer your car left and right to avoid oncoming traffic and road obstacles. Collect fuel canisters to prevent your engine from running dry. The longer you survive, the faster the road scrolls.

  • Desktop — Steer: Left / Right Arrow Keys or A / D keys
  • Desktop — Boost: Hold Spacebar when the boost meter is charged
  • Mobile: Tilt device or use on-screen left/right buttons
  • Goal: Drive as far as possible without crashing or running out of fuel

⚡ Game Features

  • Classic top-down endless racing gameplay
  • Fuel management mechanic for sustained runs
  • Boost system for overtaking and emergency evasion
  • Day/night cycle transitions as your distance increases

🏆 Pro Tips

  • Draft directly behind a car for 2 seconds to charge your boost
  • Always grab fuel canisters even when your tank is half-full
  • Stay in the center lane by default — you can dodge either direction
  • Save boost for emergency situations, not casual overtaking
