🔩 Play Neon Screws & Bolts Online

Neon Screws & Bolts is a premium, physics-based neon stick maneuvering logic puzzle game. Command a glowing magenta neon rod and navigate the security matrix grid of a cyberspace mainframe. Tap or click to unscrew yellow glowing screws and move them into empty holes to swing and pivot the stick across the board. If the stick is pinned by two screws, it remains fully locked. Pinned by only one screw, it rotates dynamically under gravity as a rigid pendulum. Removing all pins triggers a system failure crash. Guide both ends of the stick into glowing green terminal nodes to decrypt each zone. Featuring realistic rigid body angular momentum, cyan firewall bounces, dynamic noise sweep swing whooshes, locked red screw indicators, and 5 hand-crafted progressive mainframe stages.

🕹️ Visible Gameplay & Controls Guide

Tap or click a yellow screw to select and unscrew it (if not locked). The screw is then carried on the swinging end of the stick. Tap or click any empty hole within 40px of that stick end to snap and insert the screw. Maneuver both ends into pulsing green goal holes to clear the level.

💡 Expert Tips & Pro Strategies

Utilize gravity pivots: swing to build momentum, and snap when passing within 40px of cyan holes. If only one screw pins the stick, it is locked (🔒) and cannot be removed to prevent instant falling. Pay attention to gravity shifts in level 5 where gravity pulls upwards!

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why is my screw showing a lock symbol and glowing red?

A: To prevent the stick from falling down and triggering a game over, the game has 'Last-Screw Protection'. When the stick is held by only a single screw, that screw is locked (🔒) and cannot be removed until you insert the other screw elsewhere.

Q: How do the firewall collision bounces work?

A: Cyan firewalls represent physical barrier matrices. If the swinging neon stick overlaps a firewall, it undergoes an elastic damped collision rebound, reversing and damping its angular velocity to bounce realistically.

Q: What happens if both screws are removed?

A: If both screws are removed, the stick has zero pivot points and falls down under gravity, causing a system reboot crash ('STICK FELL!' fail modal). You can instantly retry the stage using the Retry button.
