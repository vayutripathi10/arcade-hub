🍾 Play Neon Bottle Shooter Online

Neon Bottle Shooter is a physics-based target shooting simulator. Launch glowing steel spheres at neon glass bottles stacked in various configurations. Master launch angles, velocity, and ricochet mechanics to break all bottles using as few shots as possible.

🕹️ Visible Gameplay & Controls Guide

Click and drag back from the launcher on desktop, or pull back with your finger on mobile to aim. Release to launch the sphere.

💡 Expert Tips & Pro Strategies

Aim for the base bottles of a stack. Collapsing the bottom row triggers a physics chain reaction that shatters the bottles above without wasting shots.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are there different bottle types?

A: Yes, introduce armored bottles (requires two hits), explosive bottles (shatters nearby targets), and moving bottle platforms.

Q: Can I preview my trajectory?

A: A dotted laser line shows your initial launch trajectory, helping you plan bank shots and angle entries accurately.

Q: Is this offline friendly?

A: Fully. Play offline on any mobile device. All physics calculations are computed locally inside the browser.
