March 30, 2026

🦖 Zen Dino 2.0: The Evolution Update

Game Update Mobile

Zen Dino just got a massive upgrade! We've listened to your feedback and rolled out version 2.0 with several highly-requested features:

  • Perfect Hitboxes: No more unfair deaths! Collision detection is now pixel-perfect, giving you much tighter control when dodging cacti.
  • Day/Night Cycle: The longer you survive, the world now seamlessly transitions from a dark starry night to a neon twilight dawn.
  • Randomized Obstacles: Cacti now spawn in different shapes and sizes—watch out for the tall ones and wide clusters!
  • Landscape Mode: Mobile users will now be prompted to flip their phones horizontally for the ultimate widescreen arcade experience.
