Neon Runner

Neon Runner is a high-octane endless runner set in a glowing cyberpunk city. Your neon parkour athlete sprints automatically at ever-increasing speed through a procedurally generated gauntlet of obstacles, pits, and moving platforms. Jump over barriers, slide under low hazards, and collect glowing coins to boost your score multiplier. Miss a dodge and it is game over — how far can you run?

🎮 How to Play

Your runner moves automatically. You control only jumping and sliding. React fast to incoming obstacles and collect as many coins as possible to maximize your final score.

  • Desktop — Jump: Spacebar or Up Arrow key
  • Desktop — Slide: Down Arrow or S key
  • Mobile — Jump: Swipe up or tap the Jump button
  • Mobile — Slide: Swipe down or tap the Slide button

⚡ Game Features

  • Endless procedurally generated obstacle courses
  • Speed increases progressively over time
  • Coin multiplier system for high-score chasers
  • Neon glow trail effects behind your runner

🏆 Pro Tips

  • Watch 2–3 obstacles ahead, not just the immediate one
  • Time double-jumps carefully — jumping too early wastes height
  • Slide under low barriers immediately when you see them approaching
  • Collect coin strings even at high speeds — they're worth the risk
