📖 How to Play
🕹️Controls: Swipe Up/Down or use Arrow Keys to switch lanes.
🪙Coins: Collect gold coins — every 10 coins refills a boost!
⚡Boost: Double Tap for 2 seconds of Invincibility!
🚧Barriers: Dodge pink barriers or get smashed!
🏆Goal: Run as far as possible and beat your best score!

About Neon Runner

Sprint across glowing platforms suspended in a futuristic neon cityscape in Neon Runner. Run, jump, and slide to avoid deadly laser spikes, moving barriers, and crumbling platforms. With highly fluid animations, precise jump physics, and a fast-paced electronic soundtrack, Neon Runner challenges you to beat your limits in an endless chase.

🕹️ Complete Game Controls Guide

On Desktop: Press Spacebar or Up Arrow to jump. Hold the button for a higher jump. Press Down Arrow to slide under low obstacles.

On Mobile: Tap the left side of your screen to slide, and tap the right side to jump. Double-tap to execute high jumps.

💡 Pro Tips & Strategies for High Scores

  • Watch your landing: Crumbling platforms fall shortly after contact. Jump off them immediately to avoid falling into the grid void.
  • Slide to clear low gaps: Some lasers hover mid-air. Time your slide early to glide underneath them safely.
  • Time jumps at the edge: To clear massive chasms, wait until the runner is at the absolute edge of a platform before leaping.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Is Neon Runner endless?
A: Yes, it is an endless running game. The speed of the runner and the frequency of obstacles scale upward the further you travel.
Q: Are there checkpoints?
A: No, Neon Runner is a classic arcade survival test. One fall or hit ends your run, pushing you to beat your high score.
