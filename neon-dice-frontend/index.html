Neon Dice Destiny

Welcome to Neon Dice Destiny, a premium 3D dice-rolling game of luck, numbers, and probability. Choose a target number, roll the high-fidelity 3D dice across 5 rounds, and see how close you can get to your locked target. A perfect score requires precision, planning, and a favor from the dice gods!

🎮 How to Play

Choose a target from 2 to 60, lock it in, and roll the dice. Your score depends on how close your cumulative total is to your target after 5 rounds.

  • Target Picker: Use the slider or the plus/minus buttons to choose a target number. Press LOCK IN TARGET to begin.
  • Roll the Dice: Tap the big ROLL DICE button to tumble the 3D dice. They will bounce, tumble, and settle.
  • Total Accumulation: Your roll values will be added to your cumulative total. Keep an eye on the progress bar!
  • Proximity Score: After round 5, you'll receive a score out of 10 based on the absolute difference between your total and target.

⚡ Technical Details

  • Procedural 3D WebGL renderer via Three.js (r128).
  • High-quality programmatic SFX via the Web Audio API (rattles, thuds, clicks, fanfares).
  • Responsive layouts with support for touch devices and viewports.
  • 100% Progressive Web App (PWA) support for offline play.

💡 Pro Tips

  • A target of 30-40 has the highest statistical probability of being reached over 5 rounds (average roll is 7).
  • Targets below 10 or above 55 are extremely difficult and constitute "Legendary Challenges"!
  • Watch the green, yellow, and red probability lights on each roll to see if you are on track.
  • Share your high scores and funny quotes with your friends on WhatsApp and X!

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is Neon Dice Destiny free to play?

Yes, 100% free with no registrations, sign-ups, or installations required.

How does it work offline?

Once loaded, the Service Worker caches all assets, allowing you to play offline on planes, trains, or subways.
