Neon Brick Breaker

Neon Brick Breaker is an action-packed cyberpunk remake of the classic Atari Breakout. Guide a glowing paddle to bounce laser balls into rows of vibrant neon bricks. Collect powerful drop power-ups including wide paddle extenders, multi-ball spawns, laser cannons, slow-motion fields, and shields. With escalating stage difficulty and boss brick patterns, this is the definitive modern brick-breaker experience.

🎮 How to Play

Move the paddle left and right to bounce the ball upward into the brick grid. Clear all bricks to complete the stage. Collect power-ups that drop from broken bricks for game-changing abilities.

  • Desktop: Move the mouse left/right to control the paddle; press Space or click to fire lasers
  • Mobile: Touch and drag left/right along the bottom; tap the screen to fire lasers (autofires automatically when lasers are active)
  • Launch: Click, tap, or press Space to launch the ball at the start of each round
  • Goal: Break all bricks without letting the ball fall off the bottom

⚡ Game Features

  • Multi-ball, laser, and wide-paddle power-ups
  • Indestructible silver bricks requiring multiple hits
  • Stage transition animations with level-up mechanics
  • Moving bricks that appear in later stages

🏆 Pro Tips

  • Hit bricks with the edge of the paddle for steep low-angle bounces
  • Prioritize the Multi-Ball power-up — it doubles your destruction output
  • Let the ball reach the top and bounce inside the upper rows for chain clears
  • Never chase power-ups off-screen — losing a life costs more than any drop

Yes, completely free in your browser. No downloads or accounts required.

Yes — swipe left/right along the bottom to control the paddle on any touch device.
