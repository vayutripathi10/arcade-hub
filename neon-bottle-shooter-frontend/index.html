Neon Bottle Shooter

Neon Bottle Shooter is a precision aiming arcade game where you must shoot and shatter glowing neon bottles before your bullet limit runs out. Bottles are arranged on shelves at varying heights and distances. Each shot must count — waste bullets and the level ends in failure. Bottles become smaller, move faster, and hide behind obstacles as levels progress, demanding sharper aim and smarter shot planning.

🎮 How to Play

Aim your crosshair at the neon bottles and fire. Each bullet costs one shot from your limited supply. Shatter every bottle on the shelf to complete the level with a star rating based on accuracy.

  • Desktop — Aim: Move the mouse to position your crosshair
  • Desktop — Shoot: Left-click to fire
  • Mobile: Tap anywhere on screen to shoot at that position
  • Goal: Destroy all bottles using the fewest possible bullets

⚡ Game Features

  • Accuracy rating system — 3-star levels for perfect shots
  • Moving bottle targets in advanced levels
  • Ricochet shots that bounce off walls to hit hidden bottles
  • Satisfying neon glass shatter effects on every hit

🏆 Pro Tips

  • Always aim for grouped bottles to shatter multiple in one shot
  • Use wall ricochets to reach bottles behind cover
  • Take your time on aiming — there is no time pressure, only bullet count
  • Watch for wobbling bottles — they indicate a moving target pattern
