💡 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How can I edit PDF text online for free?

Our online PDF Editor lets you edit PDF text directly in your browser. Double-click on any text line on the preview canvas to change the text content. You can also cover text with redact (blackout) or whiteout boxes and place new custom text labels using different font sizes and colors.

How do I sign a PDF online securely?

Navigate to the Sign tool inside the editor tab. You can draw your signature using a mouse/touchpad, type your name using handwriting fonts (Sacramento, Caveat, Great Vibes), or upload an image/PDF signature file. Our tool filters out white backgrounds automatically to output transparent signature stamps.

How can I convert PDF pages to JPEG/PNG images?

Use the "PDF to Image" tab to render PDF pages into PNG or JPEG format at normal (1.5x) or high-density (2.5x) resolution. You can download individual page images or package all converted pages into a single zipped folder bundle instantly.

Is it safe to merge, split, and edit PDFs online?

Yes! Unlike other sites that upload files to remote servers, Arcade Hub's PDF Tools operate 100% client-side. All processing (rendering, text modifications, signatures, conversions, and compilation) occurs in-memory inside your local web browser. Your private documents never leave your computer.

How do I combine images into a single PDF?

Select the "Images to PDF" tab, upload your images (JPG, PNG, WebP), and drag to rearrange their sequence. You can choose output page sizes (Fit Image, A4, US Letter) and orientations (Portrait, Landscape) before compiling them into a single PDF document.

Can I split specific pages from a PDF document?

Yes. Our "Split PDF" tab displays a visual grid of all document pages. You can manually select individual pages (e.g. 1, 3, 5) or input custom ranges (e.g. 1-3, 5-8) to extract pages and compile a brand-new PDF file instantly.
