Neon Swing

Neon Swing is an elegant physics-based swinging game where you play as a glowing orb flying through a neon skyline. Click to anchor a rope to the nearest grapple point, then release at the perfect moment to fly forward and grab the next anchor. Miss your timing and you plummet. Chain perfect swings together for speed bonuses, collect mid-air coins, and cover as much distance as possible in this graceful acrobatic challenge.

🎮 How to Play

Click or tap to shoot a rope to the nearest anchor point above you. Hold to swing, then release to fly forward. Time your release at the peak of your swing arc for maximum distance.

  • Desktop: Click and hold to latch rope; release to let go and fly
  • Mobile: Touch and hold to latch; release to fly
  • Coins: Fly through glowing coins mid-swing for score bonuses
  • Goal: Swing as far as possible without falling below the screen

⚡ Game Features

  • Realistic rope-swing physics engine
  • Procedurally placed grapple points for infinite variety
  • Speed combo multiplier for chained perfect swings
  • Neon particle trail follows your flight path

🏆 Pro Tips

  • Release at the peak of your forward arc for maximum horizontal distance
  • Aim for high anchor points — longer ropes build more swing momentum
  • Don't panic-click; wait for the right anchor before latching
  • Chain 5+ perfect swings to activate the speed multiplier bonus
