🕸️ Play Neon Swing Online

Neon Swing is a high-velocity, physics-based horizontal swing adventure set in a pulsing vector cyber-grid. Connect your grappling lines to glowing anchors, build momentum, and soar through complex architectural mazes to collect secure data packets. Featuring responsive mouse and touch hooks, smooth particle trails, and dynamic gravity grids, it combines classic physics-based fun with premium arcade aesthetics.

🕹️ Visible Gameplay & Controls Guide

Click and hold the mouse button or touch and hold the screen to grapple onto the nearest anchor point. Release to let go and soar forward. Time your hooks perfectly to build speed.

💡 Expert Tips & Pro Strategies

Grapple anchors when you are slightly below them to maximize forward swing momentum. Release at the peak of your swing arc for optimal launch height. Practice consecutive rapid grapples to clear obstacles safely.
