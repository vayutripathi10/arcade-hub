👊 Play Neon Brawler Online

Neon Brawler is an action-packed 2D cyberspace fighter. Build combo streaks to trigger Auto-Berserk mode, collect falling Katana and Laser Staff weapon chests, and execute crescent combo finishers to defeat the massive Giant Brawler Boss. Pure Left/Right action!

🕹️ Visible Gameplay & Controls Guide

Strike left with ArrowLeft/A, strike right with ArrowRight/D. Reach a 15 combo streak to trigger Auto-Berserk mode (double range + shockwaves). Collect Katana and Laser Staff weapon drops every 20 kills. Execute 3 consecutive attacks in one direction to unleash a Crescent shockwave wave. Pure left/right actions only.

💡 Expert Tips & Pro Strategies

Keep your combo streak alive to trigger Auto-Berserk. Use weapon drops (Katana and Laser Staff) and 3-hit combo finishers to deal massive knockback and damage to clear the arena.
