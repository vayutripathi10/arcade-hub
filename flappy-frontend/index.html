📖 How to Play
🕹️Controls: Press SPACE or Tap the screen to flap.
🚧Navigate: Fly through the gaps in the neon pipes.
⬇️Gravity: The bird constantly falls — keep tapping to stay airborne!
💀Don't: Hit the pipes or the ground!
🏆Goal: Pass as many pipes as possible to earn your high score!

About Neon Flappy

Neon Flappy is a high-speed synthwave adaptation of the classic flappy arcade formula. Control a glowing cyber-bird and navigate through a series of pulsing, neon pipe obstacles. The game features interactive background elements, custom sound effects, and high-speed vertical velocity challenges that test your precision and reflexes with every single tap.

🕹️ Complete Game Controls Guide

On Desktop: Press the Spacebar or Click the mouse to flap wings and gain altitude. Release to fall.

On Mobile: Tap anywhere on the screen to flap wings and rise. Good timing is key to navigating the tight spaces.

💡 Pro Tips & Strategies for High Scores

  • Find your rhythm: Rhythmic, steady taps are far more effective than erratic panic-clicking.
  • Anticipate gravity: The cyber-bird falls quickly. Flap slightly before you enter a gap to ensure you don't clip the bottom pipe.
  • Use the center visual guide: Keep the bird centered in the screen as much as possible between obstacles.
