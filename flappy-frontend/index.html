Zen Flappy

Zen Flappy is a neon cyberpunk reimagining of the classic Flappy Bird. Guide a glowing neon bird through an endless gauntlet of electrified tube barriers by tapping to flap. Each tap gives one burst of lift against constant gravity. Threading through narrow gaps requires perfect rhythm and zen-like patience. Simple controls, brutal difficulty, and one-more-try addictiveness make Zen Flappy an instant classic.

🎮 How to Play

Tap or click to flap your wings and gain lift. Release and gravity pulls you down. Thread through the gaps in each pair of neon pipe barriers. Hitting any barrier or the ground ends the run immediately.

  • Desktop: Click the mouse or press Spacebar to flap
  • Mobile: Tap anywhere on screen to flap
  • Goal: Pass through as many pipe gaps as possible
  • Scoring: +1 point for each gap successfully passed

⚡ Game Features

  • Classic flap-and-gravity physics engine
  • Randomized gap heights for infinite replayability
  • Neon visual skin with glowing particle trails
  • Local high-score board to track your best runs

🏆 Pro Tips

  • Flap in short, controlled bursts — over-flapping sends you into the top pipe
  • Aim for the center of each gap, not just getting through
  • Build a steady rhythm: consistent flap timing beats erratic tapping
  • Look one gap ahead, not at the current pipe, to plan your trajectory
