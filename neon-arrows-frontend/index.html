Neon Arrows

Neon Arrows is an original spatial logic puzzle game designed to test your sequence planning and geometric reasoning. The grid is packed with glowing neon arrow blocks. By tapping a block, you slide it forward in the direction of the arrow. If the path is clear, the block slides off the board. Can you find the perfect sequence of moves to untangle the board, navigate obstacle walls, use redirection rotators, and clear all the arrow blocks?

🎮 How to Play

Your goal is to completely clear the grid. Tap blocks to slide them off the screen. If a block is blocked, it won't move, so you must carefully determine the order of operations.

  • Tap to Slide: Click or tap on any block to slide it in the direction of its arrow.
  • Obstacle Walls: Watch out for pink dashed barriers! Blocks cannot slide through obstacle walls.
  • Sticky Rotators: Slide a block into a cyan dashed rotator tile, and it stops on that cell and changes its direction to match the rotator's arrow!
  • Collision Indicator: If you tap a blocked block, it wiggles, showing you which obstacle is in the way.
  • Moves Limit: Try to clear the board in the minimum possible moves to achieve the "Perfect" trophy.

⚡ Technical Details

💡 Pro Tips

  • Look at the outer edges of the grid first. Lines close to the borders are usually the first ones that can exit.
  • Work backwards: identify which lines are blocking your desired exits, and find a way to clear them first.
  • Use the Undo button if you make a mistake; it does not count against your score.
  • No timers: relax and plan your strategy carefully without time pressure.
