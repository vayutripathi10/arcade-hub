About Cyber Survivor

Dive into Cyber Survivor, an intense retro-themed survival game where you face off against endless waves of glowing robotic constructs and geometric hazards. Navigate a highly volatile grid space, collect energy cells to fuel your survivability, and adapt as the difficulty increases exponentially. With particle-heavy neon visuals and a highly responsive movement engine, Cyber Survivor delivers a premium classic arcade challenge directly to your browser.

🕹️ Complete Game Controls Guide

On Desktop: Use the Arrow Keys or WASD to navigate in 360 degrees. Aiming and firing are automated, letting you focus entirely on spatial awareness and dodge mechanics.

On Mobile: Use the on-screen virtual joystick. Slide your thumb in any direction to smoothly pilot your cyber ship across the arena.

💡 Pro Tips & Strategies for High Scores

  • Always maintain constant velocity: Staying in one spot will quickly lead to getting surrounded by faster enemy formations.
  • Prioritize glowing green energy cells: These are vital to unlocking upgrades and staying ahead of the difficulty curve.
  • Use the boundaries: Learn to bounce or slide off grid walls to escape tight squeezes when enemies close in.
