Cyber Survivor

Cyber Survivor is an intense rogue-lite arena shooter set in a neon-drenched cyberpunk wasteland. Survive endless waves of robotic enemies that swarm you from all directions. Collect experience orbs dropped by defeated foes, level up your character, and choose from powerful weapon upgrades to build a unique build each run. Every playthrough is different — pick the right upgrades and you might just survive the night.

🎮 How to Play

Your character auto-attacks nearby enemies. You control movement only. Survive waves, collect XP, and select upgrades every time you level up. Last as long as possible.

  • Desktop — Move: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Mobile: Use the on-screen virtual joystick to move
  • Auto-attack: Your weapons fire automatically at the nearest enemy
  • Level Up: Choose one of three upgrade cards that appear on level-up

⚡ Game Features

  • Rogue-lite progression with randomized upgrade choices
  • Multiple weapon types: lasers, grenades, shields, area blasts
  • Enemy variety increases every 2 minutes of survival
  • Boss enemies appear at survival milestones

🏆 Pro Tips

  • Keep moving in circles — standing still gets you surrounded fast
  • Prioritize area-of-effect upgrades to handle dense enemy clusters
  • Pick up XP orbs quickly — they disappear after a few seconds
  • Stack speed upgrades early to stay ahead of the swarm
