Neon Brawler

Neon Brawler is a cyberpunk side-scrolling beat-em-up where you fight through relentless waves of glowing robotic enemies on the streets of a neon city. Punch, kick, and unleash devastating combo attacks to clear each arena. Dodge incoming strikes, use the environment to your advantage, and defeat elite mini-bosses guarding each district. Fast, fluid, and ferociously fun.

🎮 How to Play

Move your fighter across the arena and attack approaching enemies. Chain light attacks into heavy finishers for combo bonuses. Avoid enemy strikes or you lose health — take down all enemies to advance.

  • Desktop — Move: Arrow Keys or WASD
  • Desktop — Attack: Z for light attack, X for heavy attack
  • Mobile: Virtual D-pad to move; on-screen Attack buttons
  • Combo: Chain 3 light attacks into 1 heavy for a power combo

⚡ Game Features

  • Fluid combo attack system with light and heavy strikes
  • Multiple enemy archetypes with different attack patterns
  • District boss fights with unique mechanics every 5 waves
  • Neon particle impacts and screen-shake feedback

🏆 Pro Tips

  • Stay mobile — standing still lets enemies surround you
  • Use heavy attacks to interrupt enemy charge animations
  • Attack from behind for bonus damage on most enemy types
  • Clear the outer edges first, then converge on center enemies
