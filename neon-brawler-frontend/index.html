📖 How to Play
🕹️Controls: Tap the Left or Right half of the screen, or use Arrow Keys.
👊Attack: Hit enemies as they approach from both sides.
⏱️Timing: Strike when enemies enter your hit range for maximum combos!
💀Don't: Let enemies reach you — or you lose!
🏆Goal: Build the longest combo streak possible!

About Neon Brawler

Step into the neon grid and unleash retro street combat in Neon Brawler. Defeat waves of incoming geometric grid warriors using rapid-fire combos, blocks, and devastating special moves. Featuring smooth action animations, particle hit effects, and an escalating difficulty curve, Neon Brawler brings the classic arcade beat-'em-up experience into the modern browser era.

🕹️ Complete Game Controls Guide

On Desktop: Use the Left and Right Arrow Keys (or A/D) to move. Press Down Arrow (or S) to strike. Use Space to block incoming attacks.

On Mobile: Use the on-screen left and right control pads to move your fighter. Tap the Strike and Block buttons on the right to battle.

💡 Pro Tips & Strategies for High Scores

  • Chain your strikes: Tap the attack button rapidly to chain simple kicks into high-damage punches and roundhouse finishers.
  • Use the block mechanic: Don't just attack blindly. Blocking blocks 100% of standard enemy damage and lets you counter-attack safely.
  • Manage crowd control: Keep all enemies on one side of your screen. Getting sandwiched between two foes will drain your health quickly.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Are there boss fights in Neon Brawler?
A: Yes, every set number of standard enemies defeated will summon a massive boss with unique attack patterns and enhanced health pools.
Q: Is Neon Brawler optimized for mobile devices?
A: Yes! The game features custom virtual layout buttons that make combat comfortable on any touch screen.
