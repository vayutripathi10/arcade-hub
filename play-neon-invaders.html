👾 Play Neon Invaders Online

Neon Invaders brings classic space invaders retro action to the neon age. Defend the coordinate grid from cascading waves of glowing geometric invaders. Fire laser arrays from behind protective shields, dodge alien plasma bolts, and shoot down high-speed bonus mechs. Featuring particle effects, screen shakes, and dynamic audio oscillators.

🕹️ Visible Gameplay & Controls Guide

Use A/D or Arrow keys on desktop to move, and SPACEBAR to fire. Tap on-screen left/right arrow buttons and the fire button on mobile screens.

💡 Expert Tips & Pro Strategies

Shoot the high-speed motherships that pass along the top margin of the screen to earn massive score bonuses. Use protective barriers wisely — alien lasers destroy them block by block.
