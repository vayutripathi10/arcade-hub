Zen Tic-Tac-Toe

Zen Tic-Tac-Toe is a sleek, neon-styled take on the timeless strategy classic. Play against a smart AI opponent or challenge a friend in local two-player mode. The AI uses a neural decision tree to adapt its strategy — it blocks your winning moves, sets up traps, and forces you to think multiple moves ahead. Clean animations, satisfying win effects, and infinite replayability make this the most polished Tic-Tac-Toe available in a browser.

🎮 How to Play

Take turns placing your symbol (X or O) on the 3x3 grid. The first player to get three of their symbols in a row — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally — wins the round.

  • Desktop: Click any empty grid cell to place your symbol
  • Mobile: Tap any empty grid cell to place your symbol
  • vs AI: You play as X; the AI plays as O automatically after your move
  • 2 Player: Players alternate tapping/clicking on the same device

⚡ Game Features

  • Adaptive AI that blocks your wins and sets up its own traps
  • Local 2-player mode on the same device
  • Win streak tracking across multiple rounds
  • Neon glow highlight on the winning three-in-a-row combination

🏆 Pro Tips

  • Always take the center square on your first move if available
  • Corner openings are stronger than edge openings against the AI
  • Create a fork: set up two winning lines simultaneously to force a win
  • Watch the AI's second move — it reveals its intended trap direction
