📖 How to Play
🕹️Controls: Use Arrow Keys or WASD on desktop. D-Pad or Swipe on mobile.
🍎Eat: Guide the snake to the glowing food to grow longer.
💀Don't: Hit the walls or eat your own tail!
⚡Speed: The snake accelerates as you eat more food.
🏆Goal: Eat as much as possible and beat your high score!

About Zen Snake

Zen Snake is a gorgeous neon-cyberpunk modernization of the beloved arcade classic. Navigate a glowing snake around a highly responsive grid arena. Eat energy orbs, grow longer, and dodge your own tail along with the hazardous outer grid walls. Featuring premium particle trails and responsive movement, Zen Snake is pure zen arcade bliss.

🕹️ Complete Game Controls Guide

On Desktop: Use the Arrow Keys (Up, Down, Left, Right) or WASD to instantly change the direction of your snake.

On Mobile: Swipe your finger in any direction (Up, Down, Left, Right) anywhere on the touch screen to guide the snake.

💡 Pro Tips & Strategies for High Scores

  • Plan your path: Don't just head straight for energy orbs. Make sure your snake's tail has a clear escape route before locking in a path.
  • Hug the walls: Traveling along the outer borders of the grid helps keep the central space clear, giving you more room as you grow longer.
  • Avoid rapid U-turns: Making double turns in high-speed modes is risky. Plan wide, circular movements to stay safe.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Does Zen Snake have touch controls?
A: Yes, Zen Snake has a highly optimized mobile swipe-detection engine for seamless gameplay on any touch device.
Q: Is the speed constant?
A: As your snake grows longer, the game speed increases slightly, requiring faster reflexes and tighter steering.
