Zen Snake

Zen Snake is a gorgeous neon-cyberpunk modernization of the beloved classic snake game. Navigate a glowing serpent around a responsive grid arena, eating energy orbs to grow longer while avoiding your own tail and the outer grid walls. The snake accelerates as it grows, turning a relaxed start into a frantic reflex challenge at high lengths. Clean mechanics, beautiful glow effects, and a deeply satisfying high-score loop.

🎮 How to Play

Steer the snake using direction controls. Guide it to eat glowing energy orbs to grow longer. Avoid hitting the walls or your own body. The longer you grow, the faster the snake moves.

  • Desktop: Arrow Keys or WASD to change direction
  • Mobile: Swipe in any direction or use the on-screen D-pad
  • Goal: Eat as many orbs as possible and achieve the highest score
  • Game Over: Hitting a wall or your own body ends the run

⚡ Game Features

  • Classic snake gameplay with neon cyberpunk visual skin
  • Progressive speed increase as your snake grows
  • Glowing particle trail behind the snake's body
  • Local high-score tracking with share-to-social buttons

🏆 Pro Tips

  • Hug the outer walls early on to keep the center space clear for growth
  • Plan your path 3–5 moves ahead — reactive play fails at high speeds
  • Avoid rapid U-turns; make wide, sweeping curves instead
  • When cornered, spiral inward rather than making panic turns
