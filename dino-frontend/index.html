Zen Dino

Zen Dino is a neon-retro endless runner inspired by the beloved classic offline dinosaur game. Sprint through a glowing cyberpunk desert, leaping over cacti and ducking under low-flying pterodactyls with perfectly timed jumps and double-jumps. The longer you survive, the faster the world scrolls, making each session an escalating reflexes test. Simple to start, impossible to master — just one more run.

🎮 How to Play

Your dino runs automatically. Press jump to leap over ground obstacles and duck to avoid flying ones. A well-timed double-jump clears large obstacles and gives you extra air time to plan your next move.

  • Desktop — Jump: Spacebar or Up Arrow key
  • Desktop — Duck: Down Arrow or S key
  • Desktop — Double Jump: Press Jump a second time while in the air
  • Mobile: Tap to jump, swipe down to duck; double-tap for double jump

⚡ Game Features

  • Classic endless runner gameplay with neon cyberpunk visuals
  • Double-jump mechanic for clearing wide obstacle gaps
  • Day-to-night visual transitions as speed increases
  • Local high-score tracking to beat your personal best

🏆 Pro Tips

  • Jump early rather than late — late jumps clip the top of obstacles
  • Save double-jump for clustered obstacle combinations
  • Watch the horizon line for pterodactyl shadows giving early warning
  • Find a rhythm: the obstacle timing becomes patterned at each speed tier
