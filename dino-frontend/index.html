📖 How to Play
🕹️Controls: Press SPACE or Tap the screen to jump.
🦘Double Jump: Press again while in the air for a second jump!
🌵Avoid: Dodge cacti on the ground and flying birds in the air.
🌙Day & Night: The world cycles between day and night as you score higher.
🏆Goal: Survive as long as possible and beat your high score!

About Zen Dino

Experience Zen Dino, a premium neon-retro reimagining of the classic browser offline dinosaur runner. Set in a glowing cyber-desert, Zen Dino introduces double-jump mechanics, day/night cycles, and high-fidelity retro audio. Help the glowing neon T-Rex dodge obstacles and survive in an endless, fast-paced chase that scales in speed as you score higher.

🕹️ Complete Game Controls Guide

On Desktop: Press Spacebar or Up Arrow to jump. Press again while in mid-air to execute a double jump.

On Mobile: Tap anywhere on the touchscreen to jump. Double-tap to execute the double-jump maneuver.

💡 Pro Tips & Strategies for High Scores

  • Time your double-jumps carefully: Wait until the peak of the first jump to trigger the second, allowing you to clear wide cactus clusters.
  • Watch for flying birds: Some obstacles appear in the air. Use a low single jump or run underneath them to stay safe.
  • Day/Night transition: Be prepared for sudden speed increases as the glowing desert environment shifts between cycles.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: What is the double jump mechanic in Zen Dino?
A: It allows you to jump a second time while in mid-air, which is essential for clearing the largest obstacles that appear in later stages.
Q: Is there a download required for Zen Dino?
A: No, Zen Dino plays instantly in any modern web browser on both mobile and desktop.
