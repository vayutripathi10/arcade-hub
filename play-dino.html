🦖 Play Zen Dino Online

Zen Dino is an endless runner inspired by the classic browser offline game. Run across a glowing neon desert grid, jump over digital cactuses, and dodge incoming pterodactyls. Build score multipliers by performing double-jumps, and enjoy cozy, beautiful graphics.

🕹️ Visible Gameplay & Controls Guide

Press SPACEBAR or UP Arrow to jump. Press down arrow to duck. Tap mobile screen to jump, or double-tap to double-jump.

💡 Expert Tips & Pro Strategies

Master the double-jump! Press jump at the absolute peak of your first jump to clear wide cactus clusters safely.

⚡ Cybernetic Specifications

  • Rendering Core: HTML5 Canvas / 2D WebGL execution vectors.
  • Framerate Limits: Locked 60 FPS up to fluid 120 FPS high-refresh capabilities.
  • Audio Synthesizer: Browser Web Audio API oscillator nodes for live programmatic synthesizer soundtrack rendering.
  • Offline Capabilities: Progressive Web App caching via local Service Workers — 100% playable offline!

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does the speed increase over time?

A: Yes, the desert grid scrolls faster as your score grows, testing your timing and reactions at higher speeds.

Q: Are there power-ups?

A: Gather glowing stars to trigger a 'Magnet' power-up that pulls in all score tokens automatically for a brief time.

Q: Can I play offline?

A: Yes! True to its offline origins, Zen Dino is fully cached via PWA and designed to run completely without internet access.
